About Find Your Anchor

Find Your Anchor is a web application designed to help college students find clubs and activities that interest them. The application is designed to be used by students, school administrators, and club leaders. Students can take an interactive quiz to find clubs that match their interests, or they can browse clubs and activities by school. School administrators can add, edit, and delete clubs and activities, and club leaders can add, edit, and delete their own clubs and activities.
